Detailed Explanations

Question 1

Correct Answer: D (Managerial)

Explanation: Managerial controls include threat assessment processes and risk management activities.

Question 2

Correct Answer: B (Technical)

Explanation: Technical controls include firewalls, encryption, and access control lists.

Question 3

Correct Answer: C (Integrity)

Explanation: Integrity is protected by hashing algorithms and integrity monitoring solutions.

Question 4

Correct Answer: B (Operational)

Explanation: Operational controls include user access reviews and log monitoring.

Question 5

Correct Answer: D (Compensating)

Explanation: Compensating controls are alternatives when primary controls cannot be implemented.

Question 6

Correct Answer: D (Corrective)

Explanation: Corrective controls remediate issues after they occur, such as backup restoration.

Question 7

Correct Answer: B (Deterrent)

Explanation: Deterrent controls discourage attackers, such as warning signs and visible cameras.

Question 8

Correct Answer: D (Preventive)

Explanation: Preventive controls stop issues before they occur, such as firewalls and encryption.

Question 9

Correct Answer: D (Detective)

Explanation: Detective controls identify events after they occur, such as IDS and audits.

Question 10

Correct Answer: A (Physical)

Explanation: Physical controls include fences, lighting, locks, and cameras.

Question 11

Correct Answer: C (Data at Rest)

Explanation: Data at rest is protected by full-disk encryption and access controls.

Question 12

Correct Answer: A (Data in Transit)

Explanation: Data in transit is protected by TLS/SSL, VPN, and encrypted protocols.

Question 13

Correct Answer: D (Data in Use)

Explanation: Data in use is protected by app-level encryption and secure enclaves.

Question 14

Correct Answer: D (Nonrepudiation)

Explanation: Nonrepudiation is provided by digital signatures and audit trails.

Question 15

Correct Answer: D (Identity Theft)

Explanation: Identity theft risks include exposure of PII such as SSNs and medical records.

Question 16

Correct Answer: A (Gap Analysis)

Explanation: Gap analysis reviews objectives versus actual controls.

Question 17

Correct Answer: C (Compliance Risk)

Explanation: Compliance risk involves violating legal or regulatory requirements.

Question 18

Correct Answer: B (Strategic Risk)

Explanation: Strategic risk threatens the organization's ability to meet goals.

Question 19

Correct Answer: A (Financial Risk)

Explanation: Financial risk involves monetary damage from breaches.

Question 20

Correct Answer: A (Directive)

Explanation: Directive controls inform people what to do, such as policies and procedures.

Exam Notes

Tip:

Review the explanations for each answer to understand why the correct choice is best. Focus on control categories, types, and risk impacts for the exam.
